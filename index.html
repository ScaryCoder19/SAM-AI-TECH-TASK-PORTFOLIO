I'm Moxit Shah, a final-year Computer Engineering student at SVIT Vasad. I got into full stack development because I wanted to follow an idea all the way — from an empty file to something running in a browser that someone can actually use.

Most of my projects sit somewhere between the frontend and some kind of backend logic — whether that's a database, an API, or an AI model doing something genuinely useful. I've built a browser music player, a todo app, an AI mental health chatbot called ClearMinds, and I'm currently working on an AI-based traffic management system.

I care about writing code that's clean, building UIs that don't get in the way, and learning by actually making things rather than just following tutorials. When I'm stuck, I figure it out — that's kind of the job.
